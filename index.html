Daily Schedule

Morning (Waking up until Lunch at 3 PM)

Wake up: Take it slow if you need; enjoy some easy time to start the day. Use this to settle in and prep mentally.

Post-breakfast (around 11 AM): Gentle productivity or light tasks, maybe brainstorming for your projects or organizing your space. This way, you're easing into work mode without diving in fully.

Afternoon (After Lunch, 3 PM - 6 PM)

  • 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Project 1 Kickoff

    Start with Project 1 and break it down into a few manageable tasks. Focus on gathering resources, making an outline, or setting a foundation.

  • 4:15 PM - 5:15 PM: Project 2 Kickoff

    Shift focus to Project 2 to keep the momentum going. Make notes, outline objectives, and get an overview. Take a 5-10 min break if needed between projects.

  • 5:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Meditation

    Give yourself a breather with meditation to relax, reset, and avoid burnout before the evening.

Evening (6 PM - 10 PM)

  • 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Certification Course Part 1

    Focus on completing a section of your course. Remember, there’s no rush; go at a pace that’s comfortable. A full hour here should help you make good progress.

  • 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM: Workout + Audiobook

    Use this time for your evening workout while listening to your audiobook. It’s a great way to combine mental and physical activity, keeping both engaging and light.

  • 9:00 PM: Wind Down

    Free time to relax and reflect on your day.
